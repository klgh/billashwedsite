Our Engagement

Well, the week of my 21st Birthday, Bill told me that he was going to talk to my parents in about a week to ask them if he could marry me. Little did I know, this stinker actually talked to my parents well in advance. I knew Bill and I were planning on going out to an early lunch for my Birthday. He had a gig later that night, so we planned this ahead of time. Bill picked me up and took me to Macaroni Grill. Funny enough, they had a special on their wine, so we ended up ordering a bottle and enjoyed a glass with our meal. After we finished eating, I assumed we would have to go back home because Bill needed to drive to his gig and I had plans to go out to dinner with my family. Instead, Bill asked me if I wanted to go to a park and walk around. This is one of our favorite things to do. Bill and I love to be outdoors whether it is a park or a random trail, so as you can guess, of course I wanted to go. I ended up picking Sims Lake Park in Suwanee, GA. This is my favorite park. When I was nannying out in Suwanee, I spent many days taking Christian to the park. They have a wonderful path around a scenic pond. This has always been one of my favorite places to relax and reflect.

So, we started walking around the path, hand-in-hand, talking about all sorts of things. Bill made fun of me because I have an eye for spotting turtles. In the lake, there is always this particular spot where the turtles like to hangout. I always find it fascinating and a story worth sharing with the little ones. As we walked around, we made our way to the other side of the lake, when Bill stopped me. I couldn’t register what was happening until he got down on his knee and asked me to marry him. Out of shock, I said, “Wait, seriously? YES!” I wasn’t expecting him to ask because he just told me he was going to talk to my parents later. I couldn’t even talk for the first couple minutes. I am pretty sure my face was frozen with a smile.

This is one of the happiest moments of my life!
Right after, we decided to bring some champagne to my house and celebrate with the family before Bill had to leave for his gig.
I am happy to share this love story with you! We can’t wait to experience and live out this new stage in our lives.
Love,
Ashley
